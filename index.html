    HotJupiter

    A "Hot Jupiter" is a type of exoplanet that's big and gas-filled, kind of like our Jupiter. But unlike Jupiter, which is far from the Sun, Hot Jupiters are very close to their stars, making them super hot! Because they orbit so near, these planets can be hundreds of times hotter than Earth, with surface temperatures reaching thousands of degrees.
